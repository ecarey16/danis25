More Deets on Da Pasta Class

With a glass of prosecco in hand and charcuterie platters at your fingertips, guests are encouraged to learn and create in a hands-on pasta making experience in ne timeas restaurant group's event space, The Upstairs. These interactive classes offer techniques for making fresh pasta. Guests will practice mixing dough, explore making pasta, with a hand-cranked pasta machine as well as shaping and filling stuffed pasta. At the end of class, participants will sit down to a three-course dinner prepared by flour + water, including wines from the regions discussed in the class. Participants will leave with fresh pasta to cook at home and a recipe book to follow.
